Goa, an emerald land, is a 'state' of mind. A mind that is completely relaxed, content and jubilant. Located on the West Coast of India in the Konkan Region, Goa is a major tourist attraction for domestic and foreign tourists alike. Water sports, river cruises, ayurvedic massage centres, live music, restaurants, mouth watering sea food, Goa has it all.
Come, explore the endless stretches of white sand, palm fringed beaches, brightly painted houses and Portugese heritage.
